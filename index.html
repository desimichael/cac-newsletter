Holiday Programs at CAC

 
 
holiday gifts
Holiday Gifts for Kids - applications close soon!

CAC helps you give your children a gift for the holidays by matching your family anonymously with a donor from our community. You provide us a wish list and we make sure that you can receive the gift in time to wrap it and and share a holiday moment with your children.

Applications are closing soon, so please sign up as soon as possible. If you have already applied to the program, please do not sign up again. If you have any questions, please contact CAC at wecanhelp@gmail.com or call (470) 456-3386.

Holiday Food

Enjoy some extra food for your family during the holiday season! Starting November 20 and running through December, CAC will have holiday food boxes available at all three CAC Mini-Market locations. One box per family, while supplies last.

Available for all CAC clients starting November 20 - no additional registration necessary.

Help CAC share what we do

If you are willing to talk about your experience with CAC either as a private interview or live with local media, government organizations, and/or groups of donors, we would love your help. Sharing your story can help us reach more neighbors in need and receive more funds for our emergency assistance program.

We'd make sure to work around your schedule and have your comments be as personal or anonymous as you'd like. Thank you for helping CAC grow!

Funding for CAC is provided in part by the City of Shoemocker, the City of Dunwoody, Shoe County Human Services, and the United Way of Greater Atlanta.

The Community Assistance Center (CAC) brings together the Shoemocker communities to provide compassionate assistance to neighbors in need by providing financial support, helping to meet basic needs, and promoting self-reliance. CAC works to prevent homelessness and hunger locally with the support of 28 congregations of all faiths, public and private grants, civic organizations, schools, businesses, and individuals. Supporters provide financial resources, in-kind donations, and volunteer manpower.
